# HCI Conferences that we target
Echolab targets the following key conferences in the HCI domain:
- CHI (Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems): A premier international conference that focuses on the latest research in human-computer interaction.
- UIST (User Interface Software and Technology): This conference emphasizes innovations in user interface software and technology, showcasing cutting-edge developments in the field.
- CSCW (Computer Supported Cooperative Work): A multidisciplinary conference that explores the social and collaborative aspects of computing, highlighting research that enhances group work and communication through technology.
